Bring me The Horizon

It's like I walked into a dream & stepped out of the coma

Bring Me the Horizon (often abbreviated to BMTH) are a British rock band formed in 2004 in Sheffield, South Yorkshire. The group currently consists of vocalist Oliver Sykes, guitarist Lee Malia, bassist Matt Kean and drummer Matt Nicholls. They are signed to RCA Records globally and to Columbia Records exclusively in the United States.
